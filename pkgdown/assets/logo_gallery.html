pigauto — Logo Gallery

Four colour variants of the hex sticker. The design: a pink pig in driving goggles pilots a cartoon car — pig + auto. The front grille is a mini-cladogram; the gold wheels echo neural-network leaf nodes. Pick your favourite, then copy it to man/figures/logo.png and run pkgdown::build_site().

To adopt a variant: copy the chosen PNG to man/figures/logo.png, then run pkgdown::build_site(). The logo appears automatically in the navbar and on the home page.

To regenerate: Rscript script/make_logo.R (requires hexSticker, ggplot2, ggforce — optionally showtext + sysfonts for the Fredoka One font).
# adopt v1 as the package logo file.copy("man/figures/logo_v1.png", "man/figures/logo.png", overwrite = TRUE) pkgdown::build_site()
