Comparative study with environmental covariates

pigauto — walkthrough being prepared

Coming soon. This walkthrough demonstrates the full pipeline for a comparative study where traits are the response and environmental variables (temperature, precipitation, habitat) are covariates. It uses the bundled delhey5809 dataset (5,809 bird species, Delhey 2019), covering Gloger's rule: the tendency for species in warmer, wetter environments to have darker plumage.

The walkthrough covers: imputation with and without covariates, multiple imputation (multi_impute()), downstream phylogenetic regression, and pooling with Rubin's rules (pool_mi()). It will appear here automatically once the precomputed results are ready.

In the meantime, see the multi-observation walkthrough for a complete worked example with observation-level covariates.
